Standard 1 - Same Day Access

This standard is about getting access to medication immediately instead of having to wait weeks, or possibly months to start.

This standard helps to remove unnecessary steps when providing medication to those who have an opioid dependency. It also helps to reduce the risk of harm by removing the wait time for medication. -+

This benefits anyone who suffers from an opioid dependency. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or any social workers you have. -+

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. -+
