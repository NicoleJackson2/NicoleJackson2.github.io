Standard 5 - Retention

This standard allows people to stay in treatment for as long as they want, as well as at key points in their life such as leaving hospital or prison.

This standard helps to keep people in treatment without any unexpected changes or unplanned discharges. It also supports keeping treatment at key points in their lives by providing it when they leave hospital or prison. - +

This benefits anyone currently receiving treatment, or anyone that might receive treatment in the future. - +

To get help with this you can contact social workers or the NHS. - +

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. - +
