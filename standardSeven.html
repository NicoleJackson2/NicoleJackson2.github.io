Standard 7 - Primary Care

This standard allows people to access their medication or support through primary care providers such as GP'S or community pharmacy.

This standard helps to create effective treatment partnerships by connecting health and social care between all areas such as hospital, primary care, prision etc. This will provide the person with a better treatment qualtiy. -+

This benefits anyone receiving treament or might receive treatment in future. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor or social workers. -+
