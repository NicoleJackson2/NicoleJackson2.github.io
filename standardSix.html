Standard 6 - Psychological Support

This standard is about highlighting the role that positive relationships play by supporting people to develop positive relationships and new ways of coping.

This helps to teach and build new coping strategies and highlight the importance of positive relationships. It helps with mental struggles which are just as important as physical, by providing support through psychological services. -+

Anyone who has drug related mental health issues. -+

To get help with this you can contact social workers or NHS. -+
