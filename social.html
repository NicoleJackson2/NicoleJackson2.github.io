Social

What is this?-+

This category is about social support and mentions all standards which relate to this, including where to get help with this.

There are standards which relate to this category.

These are:

  • Standard 6: Psychological Support
  • Standard 8: Independent Advocacy and Social Care

Standard 6: Psychological Support

What is it?

This standard is about highlighting the role that positive relationships play by supporting people to develop positive relationships and new ways of coping.

How does this help with the category?

This helps socially as it promotes positive and new relationships, giving the person new social connections.

Where to get help?

To get help with this, you can contact social workers or the NHS.

Standard 8: Independent Advocacy and Social Care

What is it?

This standard allows people to request a worker to help them with housing, welfare or income.

How does this help with the category?

This helps socially by providing a voice for the person and making sure they are heard. This can help with housing and money, which in turn could provide a better social life for the person.

Where to get help?

To get help with this, you can contact social workers, or advocacy workers.
