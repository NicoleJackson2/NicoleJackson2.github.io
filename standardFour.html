Standard 4 - Harm Reduction

This standard is about providing harm reduction to those already receiving treatment or medication. This includes: needles and syringes, BBV testing, injecting risk assessments, wound care and naloxone.

This standard helps to reduce risk to those who are receiving treatment or medical support by still providing them with harm reduction services to help them understand how to reduce harm, this could be potentially life saving. -+

This benefits anyone who may be using drugs outside their medication. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or any social workers you have. -+

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. -+
