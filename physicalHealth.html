Physical Health

What is this?-+

This category is about physical health support and mentions all standards which relate to this, including where to get help with this.

There are standards which relate to this category.

These are:

  • Standard 2: Choice
  • Standard 3: Assertive Outreach and Anticipatory Care
  • Standard 4: Harm Reduction
  • Standard 5: Retention
  • Standard 7: Primary care

Standard 2: Choice

What is it?

This standard is about a person being able to decide their own medication and dosage, after discussing it with a worker. It also allows them to make changes to the medication if cirucmstances change.

How does this help with the category?

This helps with physical health as it allows medication and dosages to be changed to what best benefits the person and their circumstances, instead of having everyone on the same.

Where to get help?

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or any social workers you have.

Standard 3: Assertive Outreach and Anticipatory Care

What is it?

This standard is about providing support to those who are thought to be high risk due to drug use.

How does this help with the category?

This helps with physical health as it provides support to people high risk who will most likely have health issues from drug use.

Where to get help?

If you think someone is at high risk and could benefit from this you can contact your local council, or any support workers.

Standard 4: Harm Reduction

What is it?

This standard is about providing harm reduction to those already receiving treatment or medication. This includes: needles and syringes, BBV testing, injecting risk assessments, wound care and naloxone.

How does this help with the category?

This helps with physical health as it teaches people harm reduction techniques that they could use on themselves or other people they may be with.

Where to get help?

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or any social workers you have.

Standard 5: Retention

What is it?

This standard allows people to stay in treatment for as long as they want, as well as at key points in their life such as leaving hospital or prison.

How does this help with the category?

This helps with physical health as it allows treatment to continue until the person is fit enough to not recieve it any more.

Where to get help?

To get help with this you can contact social workers or the NHS.

Standard 7: Primary Care

What is it?

This standard allows people to access their medication or support through primary care providers such as GP'S or community pharmacy.

How does this help with the category?

This helps with physical health as it makes accessing vital medication much easier and more accessibile.

Where to get help?

To get help with this you can contact your doctor or social workers.
