Standard 2 - Choice

This standard is about a person being able to decide their own medication and dosage, after discussing it with a worker. It also allows them to make changes to the medication if circumstances change.

This standards helps to provide a more personalised medication plan, as it has been realised the recommended dosages may be too much or too little for some people. -+

This helps anyone with a opioid dependency who needs medication prescribed. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or any social workers you have. -+

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. -+
