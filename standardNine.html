Standard 9 - Mental Health

This standard allows people to have mental health treatment alongside their drug treatment.

This standard helps to provide mental health support/treatment for those who may need it, whilst they are also receiving drug treatment. -+

This benefits anyone that is suffering from mental health issues whilst receiving drug relate treatments. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS or your social worker. -+

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. -+
