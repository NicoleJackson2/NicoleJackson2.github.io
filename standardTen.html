Standard 10 - Trauma Informed Care

This standard is to recognise that people in treatment for drug related issues may have experienced trauma which could still be impacting them.

This standard helps to provide support to people with previous trauma by providing services to help with this and keeping people within these for as long as they need. -+

This benefits anyone with previous trauma who is receiving drug related treatment. -+

To get help with this you can contact your doctor, the NHS, or your social worker. -+

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website. -+
